All about the London Underground

The London Underground was built in 1863. There are currently 11 London Lines. There are currently building works going on. In 2025, brand new Piccadilly Line trains which will be walk-through just like the Metropolitain Line.

The Metropolitain Line

The Metropolitain Line was the first ever London Underground Line to be built. It was orriginally called the Metropolitain Railway.

Shortest journey

The shortest route on the London Underground is between COVENT GARDEN and LEICESTER SQUARE. Both of these stations are on the Piccadilly Line.
